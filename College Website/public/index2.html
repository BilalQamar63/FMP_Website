ABOUT US

EDUFORT ACADEMY was founded with the main goal to help educational institutions by offering “all solutions under one umbrella” instead of having an employee. EDUFORT ACADEMY has been acclaimed as a leading educational research and education consulting company that offers end to end services from the commencement of your school to its effective operation in India for more than a decade. We are the pioneers in the field of Result improvement programmes for CBSE Syllabus Schools. A CBSE school affiliation is a crucial step forward in the growth of a school. The school's expansion is often hampered by a lack of knowledge and a clear grasp of CBSE requirements. Our services assist you in streamlining this procedure so that you can concentrate on other vital responsibilities. Our team of highly skilled professionals work around the clock to make your vision a reality. We provide plenty of Class room procedures for interactive and experiential learning in addition to solutions and support to teachers and students in the teaching learning process. We also provide curriculum designing, Student & teacher support system, Monitoring of standards and effective school management. We conduct Model Examination, Unit test and Result improvement test series in CBSE schools to improve the confidence of students through practice .We also publish Teachers Manuals, Registers and Question banks . Our question papers have helped the students to score higher grades in Public examination.
